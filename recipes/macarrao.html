Receita da Macarrao

Buttered noodles are simple to make with your favorite pasta, butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper for a quick and easy, kid-friendly dish. Fresh herbs and a little lemon juice could be added to amp up the flavor. Perfect to serve either as-is or alongside steak, chicken, or meatballs. It's such a delicious recipe, yet I get many questions on how to make it.

Ingredientes

  • Macarrao
  • Manteiga
  • Queijo parmesao
  • Sal e pimenta

Passo a passo

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
